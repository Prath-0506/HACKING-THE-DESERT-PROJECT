About Ecozone

Ecozone is a community-driven platform encouraging you to recycle plastic, share resources, plant trees, and build a greener world together.

How It Works

1. Recycle Plastic

Upload proof of recycling and earn points.

2. Share Food & Items

Donate extra food and unused items to people nearby.

3. Carpool & Plant Trees

Reduce emissions with carpooling and use your points to plant real trees.
